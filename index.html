Did you know?

Modular synthesizers consist of a collection of individual modules wired to a power board. Each module performs a different function such as sound generation, mixing, amplification, filtering, or signal splitting. Modular synthesizer performers can customize their synthesizer by adding or removing modules based on their needs.
